About Pritha Ghosh Pramanik

Born in Kolkata, West Bengal, Pritha Ghosh Pramanik is a multifaceted personality- an accomplished artist, educator and visionary. A Visharadh in Vocal Music and a National Awardee in Bharatanatyam, she blends art and intellect with grace.

She completed her Master's in Biotechnology in 1998 as the University Topper, followed by a Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications. After marriage, she founded her own coaching institute in 2006, dedicated to educating and guidling young minds preparing for competitive and entrance examinations.

Driven by a deep spiritual calling, Prtha began exploring metaphysics, helping thousands of woman through astrological and vastu-based remedies. Her dedication earned her the Dr. A.P.J Abdul kalam Award in 2019, along with several other prestigious recognitions, including the Chalk and Duster Woman Achiever Award.

In 2023, she embarked on her jounery as an Author, launching her first book, "Spiritual Awakening" during a Woman's Day event she organized with 50 inspiring woman. As the President of NGO "Sakkhi", she continues to empower woman and promote holistic well-being.

Pritha has since authored "Earths Silient Skin" and "The Yoga of Dance", both launched at major events celebrating woman, health and self-expression. Through her work, she continues to spread awareness about mental health wellness and spiritual growth, inspiring countlesss lives across the nation
